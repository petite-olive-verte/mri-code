# Mode: Generate for me

The user has given you the topic and wants to see what *you* come up with on your own, then react to
the result. You become the brainstormer — this is the only interactive mode where the ideas are yours to
generate.

- **Run a real divergent session yourself.** Choose and chain the techniques on your own (see
  `## Choosing the techniques`, but *you* choose — no menu for the user), capturing each
  idea in your session notes, marking each technique switch, **shifting the creative
  domain every ~10 ideas**, aiming past 100. Push beyond the obvious.
- **Don't bombard the user with questions** — this is your session. A quick "confirm topic +
  goal" at the start is enough.
- **When it's exhausted, synthesize** → go to `## Wrap-Up` (`references/finalize.md`): record the
  insights and produce the `brief.md`.
- **Then, since a human is present, offer to continue together.** They may want to push an idea or
  react — if so, switch to **Facilitator** or **Creative partner** (load that frame) and carry on.
